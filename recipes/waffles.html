Best Waffle recipe in the world

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp of sugar

Direction

  1. Combine the flour, sugar and baking powder into a bowl
  2. Mix thouroughly with whisk
  3. Add the milk, vanilla and egg
  4. Continue to stir until the batter is smooth (about 3 minutes)
  5. The batter is ready for waffle making
